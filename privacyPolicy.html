Privacy Policy for Agrora Online Grocery Store

1.1. Personal Information: We may collect the following personal information from you:

- Name

- Payment information (credit card details, billing address)
- Account credentials (username and password)
- Order history
- Preferences and settings


1.2. Automatically Collected Information: We may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage of our services, including:

- IP address
- Device type

2.1. Providing Services: To process your orders, provide customer support, and improve your shopping experience.

2.2. To personalize and customize your experience, including product recommendations and promotions.

2.3. Communications: To send order updates, promotional emails, and marketing communications based on your preferences (you can opt out at any time).

2.4. Payment Processing: To process payments and prevent fraud.

2.5. Analytics: To analyze and improve our services, including the functionality of our website and app.

2.6. Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

2.7 Security: To protect our services and your personal information.

We may share your personal information with third parties for the following purposes:

3.1. Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in providing our services, including payment processors, delivery services, and IT support.

3.2. Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to lawful requests and protecting our rights.

5. Security
